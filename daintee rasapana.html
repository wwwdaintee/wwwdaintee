Daintee Rasa Pana

First televised in 1990, Daintee Rasa Pana is a TV show for 5-7 years olds which is broadcast every Sunday on ITN at 12:30-1:00pm. During the show 8 children aged 5-6 build up their confidence in public speaking and presentation skills by singing songs and telling the audience about their lives. Meanwhile 4 children aged 6-7 unleash their imagination and draw pictures. To apply to take part in the show, please the following to "Application for Daintee Rasa Pana, Daintee Marketing Limited, 72C Kandawala Road, Ratmalana":

* A letter containing the following information: (1) Child's full name (2) Child's birth date (3) Name of the child's school (4) Which talent the child wishes to showcase - singing or drawing (5) Names of parents (6) Telephone number (7) Address (8) Signature of Parent
* A copy of the child's birth certificate
* 2x Empty Mr Bitz packets
* 50x Empty hard boiled sweets or toffee wrappers
